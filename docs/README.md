# YOLO Markets

YOLO Markets is where internet conviction becomes a tradable market.

Instead of limiting trading to traditional assets or binary event outcomes, YOLO Markets is built around narratives, cultural topics, and attention. If a topic matters online and people have conviction about where it is going, the goal is to make that conviction tradeable.

## What This Product Is

YOLO Markets combines:

- attention-driven markets
- perp-style trading mechanics
- an order-book trading experience
- a live index that turns internet signals into a market reference price

In plain terms, users can go long or short on a topic and trade around where its attention and narrative strength are headed.

## Beta Status

YOLO Markets is currently in beta.

That matters for how the product should be understood:

- the onboarding experience is intentionally simplified
- some access may be controlled through invites
- users are automatically funded with `yCash` collateral during beta
- the points program and leaderboard are active product systems

The beta is designed to let users understand the product quickly, trade the core experience, and give feedback while the team tightens the system.
