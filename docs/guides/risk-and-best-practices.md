# Risk and Best Practices

YOLO Markets is fast and expressive. Users should approach it with discipline.

## The Main Risk Profile

These are attention-driven perp markets. That combines:

- leverage and margin risk
- funding risk
- market-liquidity risk
- narrative volatility

## Best Practices for New Users

Start with:

- small size
- topics you actually understand
- clear exit ideas

## Respect Funding

- am I paying to hold this view?
- is the market overstretched relative to the index?
- does this still make sense after funding cost?

## Respect Narrative Reflexivity

- obvious trades can become crowded
- price can move before the narrative feels "fully proven"

## The Practical Standard

Do not assume:

- beta convenience means low risk
- points justify poor entries
- strong conviction removes the need for risk management
