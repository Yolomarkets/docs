# Getting Started

This guide explains how a user gets into YOLO Markets during the current beta.

## 1. Sign In

The product uses a wallet-linked identity flow. From a user perspective, the important part is simple:

- connect or authenticate your wallet
- complete the beta access flow
- enter the trading interface

Some beta access may depend on invite status before the full trading experience is unlocked.

## 2. Beta Collateral Is Set Up For You

During beta, YOLO automatically funds users with `yCash` collateral.

That means users do not need to manually bridge or deposit the beta trading asset just to understand the product.

In practical terms:

- `yCash` is the collateral used in the current beta trading experience
- it is used as the quote-side balance that supports positions and margin
- the product can top the account up automatically when needed during onboarding

## 3. Understand What You Are Looking At

Before placing a trade, focus on a few basics:

- the market topic
- the current price context
- the index chart
- the order-book context
- your available collateral and position state

## 4. Place a Small Trade First

The best onboarding path is usually:

- pick a market you understand
- start with a small position
- watch how price, index, and PnL behave
- learn how the market reacts before sizing up

## 5. Track Your Progress

Once you are active, the main things to monitor are:

- positions
- unrealized PnL
- available margin
- funding exposure
- points and leaderboard standing
