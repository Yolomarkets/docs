# Getting Started

## 1. Sign In

- connect or authenticate your wallet
- wallets are created and managed through Privy for security
- complete the beta access flow
- enter the trading interface

## 2. `yCash` Is Set Up For You

During beta, YOLO automatically funds users with `yCash` collateral.

- `yCash` is the collateral used in the current beta trading experience
- it supports positions and margin

## 3. Understand What You Are Looking At

Before placing a trade, focus on a few basics:

- the market topic
- the index chart
- the order-book context
- your available collateral and position state

## 4. Place a Small Trade First

- pick a market you understand
- start with a small position
- watch how price, index, and PnL behave

## 5. Track Your Progress

Watch positions, PnL, available margin, funding, and points.
