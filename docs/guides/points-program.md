# Points Program

The points program is an active part of the YOLO beta experience.

## What Points Are

Points are the product's current beta incentive layer.

- reward participation
- create progression inside the product
- make activity visible through the leaderboard

## How Users Earn Points

At a high level, points are tied to trading activity.

- trading activity contributes to points
- points are tracked by the system
- leaderboard position reflects that ongoing activity

## Why It Exists in Beta

## Why It Exists

- come back
- keep participating
- compare activity with other traders

## How To Think About It

- they are real inside the current product
- they are tied to usage
- they are visible through ranking and stats
- they are not a substitute for trade quality or risk management
