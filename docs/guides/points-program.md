# Points Program

The points program is an active part of the YOLO beta experience.

## What Points Are

Points are the product's current beta incentive layer.

They exist to:

- reward participation
- create progression inside the product
- make activity visible through the leaderboard

The important thing is to treat points as a live product system, not as a promise about future tokens, rewards, or economic guarantees.

## How Users Earn Points

At a high level, points are tied to trading activity.

That means the more meaningfully a user participates in the market, the more points activity can accumulate over time. The exact reward math is intentionally not the point of these docs. What matters is the product behavior:

- trading activity contributes to points
- points are tracked by the system
- leaderboard position reflects that ongoing activity

## Why It Exists in Beta

The points system helps beta users stay engaged with the product beyond a single trade.

It gives users a reason to:

- come back
- keep participating
- compare activity with other traders

In a beta environment, this is useful because it creates a stronger feedback loop between product usage and product learning.

## How To Think About It

The right way to think about points during beta is:

- they are real inside the current product
- they are tied to usage
- they are visible through ranking and stats
- they are not a substitute for trade quality or risk management

Users should not force bad trades just to chase points. Points work best as a byproduct of informed participation.
