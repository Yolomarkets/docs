# Perps and Funding

YOLO Markets uses perp-style mechanics because they are a strong fit for live, evolving topic markets.

## What a Perp Is

A perp is a market that stays open continuously instead of expiring at a fixed date.

That matters for attention-driven markets because narratives do not resolve neatly on a calendar.

## Why Funding Exists

Funding is the mechanism that helps keep a perp market connected to its reference price.

At a high level:

- if the tradable market drifts away from its reference level
- the funding mechanism helps create pressure for it to move back toward balance

## How To Think About Funding on YOLO

On YOLO, funding should be understood as a market-balancing tool.

For traders, that means funding is not just background infrastructure. It affects holding cost and trade quality.

## What Users Should Watch

Before holding a position, users should pay attention to:

- whether the market price looks stretched versus the index
- whether they are trading a short-term move or holding longer
- whether funding will help or hurt their position if they stay in it

## The Practical Rule

If you are trading YOLO markets, do not think only about direction.

Think about:

- direction
- entry
- funding
- how long you plan to hold
