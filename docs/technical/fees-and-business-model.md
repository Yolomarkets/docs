# Fees and Business Model

YOLO Markets monetizes through trading activity.

Fees do three things:

- monetize market usage
- create treasury value for the platform
- tie business performance to actual trading demand

## Technical Reality

The stack tracks and aggregates collected fees at the exchange and engine level, then exposes a path to inspect and collect them.

## Why This Fits the Product

The model is simple: if the venue is useful, users trade, and the platform earns from that usage.
