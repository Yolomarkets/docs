# What You Can Trade

YOLO Markets is designed around attention and narrative, not just traditional financial primitives.

## The Market Shape

A YOLO market represents a topic users can be long or short.

- a cultural narrative
- an internet trend
- a meme or attention cluster

## What "Attention-Driven Perps" Means

It is a continuous market built around a topic rather than a traditional ticker.

- the market stays live instead of expiring at a fixed date
- the market has an index that reflects the topic's signal
- traders interact with it using familiar long/short perp mechanics

## What Makes These Markets Different

These markets are narrative-driven, which can make them:

- faster
- more reflexive

## What Users Should Expect

When a topic has a market on YOLO, users should expect:

- a market page with price and context
- an index that anchors the market
- a tradable long/short market with positions and PnL
